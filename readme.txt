= Schedule Product Delivery Date for WooCommerce =
People nowadays rely on online stores to purchase daily necessities such as dairy products, food, and other items to avoid the hassle of leaving their homes. In such cases, the store owners may meet difficulties in organizing product delivery plans for their regular customers.

== Installation ==
= Minimum Requirements =
* WooCommerce 5.0 or greater
* WordPress 5.7 or higher

= Automatic installation =
1. Log in to your WordPress dashboard.
2. Navigate to the Plugins menu, and click "Add New".
3. Search and locate 'Schedule Product Delivery Date for WooCommerce' plugin.
4. Click 'Install Now', and WordPress will take it from there.

= Manual installation =
Manual installation method requires downloading the 'Schedule Product Delivery Date for WooCommerce' plugin and uploading it to your web server via your FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/#manual-plugin-installation).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How to enable plan delivery on an individual product? =
<ol>
<li>Navigate to Products, choose a product edit option.</li>
<li>In the product data, enable the Plan Delivery checkbox.</li>
</ol>

= How to set different delivery settings on specific products? =
<ol>
<li>Go to Products, click 'Edit,' and enable the Plan Delivery option.</li>
<li>Disable the Global Settings by selecting the Plan Delivery tab.</li>
<li>And then, you can change the Calendar and Display settings.</li>
</ol>

= Is it possible to indicate holidays for delivery in the global calendar setting?  =
<ol>
<li>Yes, go to Products > Plan Delivery and enter every holiday or day that you won't be accessible for delivery. From the date picker provided in the Holiday/Unavailable days option, you can add or delete as many days as you like.</li>
</ol>
